About the Festival

Fusion Gourmet is a food festival born from the passion for Korean and Japanese cuisine. Over three days, world-class chefs gather to share ancestral techniques and modern creations that fuse the best of both cultures.

Each experience is designed to awaken the senses and connect attendees with the rich culinary tradition of East Asia.

Our Philosophy

  • Authenticity in every dish and technique
  • Sustainability and seasonal ingredients
  • Innovation without losing cultural roots
  • Accessible experiences for all food lovers
  • Respect for producers and their traditions
